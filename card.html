Labrador belongs to the Dog Breed Group of Sporting Dogs.It weights aprox.55 to 80 pounds and reaches 1 foot, 9 inches to 2 feet tall at the shoulder.It has smooth, short, dense and straight coat and its color ranges to black, chocolate, or yellow (pale yellow (nearly white) to fox red).The Labrador is one of the most popular breeds of dog in Canada, the United Kingdom and the U.S.It is a favourite disability assistance breed in many countries, as Labradors are frequently trained to aid the blind, those who have autism, to act as a therapy dog or to perform screening and detection work for law enforcement and other official agencies.
